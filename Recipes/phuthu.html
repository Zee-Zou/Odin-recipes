Krummelpap Recipe

Krummelpap, a variation of mieliepap, is an old-time South African favorite, probably eaten by all of the different race groups.

This krummelpap recipe was sent to us by John Herber. He was taught this method as a kid by Johanna Sithole, a Zulu lady whom he holds in very high esteem.

Ingredients:

  • maize meal
  • salt
  • water
  • amasi
  • fresh cream

Steps:

  1. Fill medium sized pot to 1/3 with hot water and bring to boiling point.
  2. Once boiling rapidly, put in a table spoon of salt.
  3. Pour in four measures of maize meal from a small mug.
  4. Stir mixture consistently at boiling point heat, with large braai-style fork.
  5. Continue mixing until maize meal is thick and in large lumps, then reduce heat to low.
  6. Repeat this stirring of the pap every 8 minutes for 1 hour 15 minutes at a low heat.
